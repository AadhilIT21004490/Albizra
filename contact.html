FAQ

  • Where does Al Bizra source its teas from?

    At Al Bizra, we take pride in sourcing our teas from renowned tea gardens and estates in Assam state, India. We work closely with trusted suppliers to ensure that we offer only the finest quality teas to our customers.

  • What types of teas does Al Bizra offer?

    We offer a wide selection of teas to cater to diverse preferences. Our range includes classic black teas, soothing green teas, aromatic herbal teas, and delightful flavored teas. Each tea is carefully curated to provide a unique and enjoyable tea experience.

  • How does Al Bizra ensure the freshness and quality of its teas?

    We prioritize freshness and quality in every aspect of our business. Our teas are sourced from reputable suppliers who share our commitment to excellence. Additionally, we carefully package our teas in airtight containers to preserve their freshness, aroma, and flavor until they reach our customers' hands.

  • Does Al Bizra offer organic or ethically sourced teas?

    Yes, we offer a selection of organic and ethically sourced teas. We understand the importance of sustainable and responsible sourcing practices, and we strive to provide teas that meet these criteria. Look for our specially labeled organic and ethically sourced teas in our product range.

  • How can I place an order with Al Bizra?

    Ordering from Al Bizra is simple and convenient. You can easily place your order by phone, WhatsApp, or email. Our friendly customer service representatives are available to assist you over the phone, where they will guide you through the available tea selections, offer recommendations if desired, and help you finalize your order. Alternatively, you can initiate a conversation with our team through WhatsApp, where they will promptly respond to your inquiries and guide you through the ordering process. If you prefer email communication, simply compose an email with your tea selections, quantities, and any specific requirements, and our dedicated team will ensure a prompt response. We prioritize personalized service and aim to make your ordering process as convenient as possible, ensuring that your tea selections are accurately processed and promptly delivered to your doorstep., our customer service team is always ready to help via phone or email.
